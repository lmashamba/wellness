The CEO & Director Of Nghonyi Wellness and Projects Pty Ltd

Nghonyama Musa currently practices as a Psychologist in a part-time private practice at Malamulele Township and in Vosloorus in Boksburg, where she offers counselling to people with psychological problems. She also specializes in working with learners with behavioural and learning problems. She is currently working as a Senior Psychologist for the department of Correctional Services. She worked as a Psychologist for SAPS under Employee Health and Wellness Unit (previously known as EAP). She facilitates team development. She is a motivational speaker and conducts workshops to different organizations on different topic.

Nghonyama Musa holds a Master’s Degree in Guidance and counselling and is registered with the HPCSA (Health Professions Council of South Africa) as an Educational Psychologist with registration number PS0106275 and currently busy trying to register with UK health profession council. Our Services

Youth Mental Health session
Youth mental health services focus on addressing the psychological, emotional, and behavioral challenges that young people face as they navigate various life stages. These services include counseling, therapy, support groups, and educational programs tailored to the unique needs of adolescents and young adults.
Mental Health Matters
Mental health is an essential aspect of overall well-being, and it matters significantly for both church members and leaders. The pressures of spiritual leadership, caregiving, and personal struggles can affect anyone in a faith community. It's crucial for churches to foster an environment that prioritizes mental health, supporting both members and leaders.
Organizational development
Is an essential process that focuses on improving both individual and collective well-being within an organization. It involves leveraging psychological principles to enhance the overall effectiveness, health, and performance of the organization by aligning its structures, behaviors, and relationships with its broader goals.
Political Campaign Support
Assisting candidates who align with our mission through strategies and outreach.
