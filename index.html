Our Vision for a Better Future

Founded with the vision to amplify the voices of the disenfranchised, the Afrikan Communist Congress ACC was created to bridge gaps in political representation and advocacy. We believe in the power of community and the necessity of a united front for real change.

Our affiliations with "ACC"

Mr. Mpumelelo Lekgeo is the Cofounder, Director, and President of the Afrikan Communist Congress (ACC), a dynamic political force dedicated to social change and community upliftment. With a political journey starting in the early 2000s, he has tirelessly worked as an activist focused on improving the lives of South Africans. Mr. Lekgeo believes that true progress stems from collective effort, prioritizing community advancement over personal interests.

Lekgeo Mpumelelo

President

Mr. Mdhluli Bekiswayo Lucky is a key player in today's political sphere, acting as the Founder, Director, and Deputy President of the Afrikan Communist Congress (ACC). This influential organization is rooted in a comprehensive sociopolitical, philosophical, and economic ideology that aligns with the principles of socialism. The ACC is dedicated to creating a communist society, aiming to construct a socioeconomic framework that emphasizes common ownership and collective prosperity.

Mdhluli Bekiswayo Lucky

Deputy President

Mrs. Buhle Precious Magagula is a dynamic young veteran and a visionary leader in the political field. As the Founder, Director, and Youth President of "Afrikan Communist Congress's (ACC) she has dedicated herself with unwavering commitment to empowering the youth and shaping the future of her community. Her journey, while at times controversial, showcases her sophisticated approach to building a trusted political identity. Through her leadership, she inspires others to engage actively in the political process while fostering growth and unity within her community.
